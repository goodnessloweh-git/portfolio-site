Community

She Builds Tech

I founded She Builds Tech because I believe that every woman who wants to get into tech deserves to see someone who looks like her doing it first.

When I started my DevOps journey, I noticed how few women were in the spaces I was learning in. I wanted to change that not just for myself, but for every woman who has ever felt like tech wasn't built for her.

She Builds Tech is a community where women in tech and women transitioning into the industry come together to share their stories, support each other, and build real skills.

"I started this community because I wanted other women to know. You don't have to have everything figured out before you begin. You just have to begin."
Loweh Goodness, Founder

We tell the stories of women who are building, shipping, and leading in tech. Because those stories matter. They show other women who are discouraged that it is possible, that there is space for them, and that they are not alone.
